Preissig said ...

For some reason the files will often get renamed when downloaded. ubuntu12.04_labs.zip.001 will often get renamed to "ubuntu12.04_labs.zip.zip," then ubuntu12.04_labs.zip.002 gets renamed to the same name, which then may show up as "ubuntu12.04_labs.zip.zip(2)" (assuming they are downloaded in order.)

It's a real annoyance, but you have to name the file names back to what they are supposed to be if your computer renames them when downloading. Then use 7-zip to unzip them.

The files have been successfully downloaded and unzipped, so they are not corrupted on the system.

--Preissig 08:33, 13 September 2012 (CDT)
