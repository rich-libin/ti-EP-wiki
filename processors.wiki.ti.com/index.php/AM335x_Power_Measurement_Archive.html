AM335x Power Consumption for different use cases[edit]

The power measurements have been performed on the earlier version of software release. The ARM clock frequency and DDR clock frequency are different than the configurations shown in the datasheet.

Dynamic voltage and frequency scaling (DVFS) and SmartReflex Adaptive Voltage Scaling (AVS) are not enabled in the software. VDD_MPU and VDD_CORE active power can be reduced by utilizing these power management techniques.
