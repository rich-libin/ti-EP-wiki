OMAP5 ARM Cortex-A15 based developer platforms[edit]

Compulab,CM-T54 System on Module[edit]

Compulab-CM-T54.jpg

CM-T54 is based on the Texas Instruments OMAP5432 application processor featuring a high performance dual-core Cortex-A15 CPU at up to 1.5GHz coupled with powerful PowerVR SGX544 graphics, video processing units and the digital signal processor (DSP) subsystem.

Product Highlights:

  • Fully featured Cortex-A15 System-On-Module (SoM) starting from $99
  • Texas Instruments OMAP5432 dual-core Cortex-A15 CPU, up to 1.5GHz
  • Up to 4GB DDR3 and up to 32GB on-board eMMC
  • Video Processing Unit, 1080p decoding and encoding
  • Integrated PowerVR SGX544 GPU with OpenGL-ES and OpenCL EP support.
  • Graphics controller with up to 3 display interfaces up to 1920 x 1200.
  • Ethernet, SATA, USB3.0, USB2.0 x3, UART x5, SDIO x3, GPIO x106
  • WiFi 802.11b/g/n and Bluetooth 3.0
  • Linux OS
  • Miniature size: 73 x 68 x 5 mm
  • Temperature range 0°C to 70°C (optional from - 40°C to 85°C).



IGEPv5: TI OMAP5432 Dual Cortex-A15 High Performance & Low Power Platform[edit]

igepv5_side.png

IGEPv5: OMAP5432 Platform Dual-Core ARM Cortex-A15. The first industrial ARM OMAP5 Platform on the market today.
The IGEPv5 is an industrial embedded platform. It is based on OMAP5 Dual Cortex-A15, DSP (TMS320DMC64x fixed 32-bit), IVA-HD (High Definition image and video hardware encoder/decoder accelerators), IPU (Cortex M4 Image Processing Unit, including two ARM Cortex M4 microprocessors for low-power offload and real-time responses), ABE (Audio back-end subsystem), ISS (Imaging subsystem consisting of image signal processor (ISP) and still image coprocessor (SIMCOP) block, 3D Graphics Processing Unit (GPU), including POWERVR SGX544 dual-core, 2D Graphics accelerator, Face detect module. The IGEPv5 platform supports until 4096 MB DDR3L and 8GB eMMC, 3 x 1080p60 video encoding/decoding, Ethernet controller (10/100/1000Mbps Gigabit Ethernet PHY controler (RJ45)), Debug Serial Console (RS232 (DB9)), 1 x USB3.0, 4 x USB 2.0, 1 x microHDMI (video+audio), 1 x Stereo Audio In, 1 x Stereo Audio Out, 1 x MicroSD Card Reader, 1 x JTAG, RTC, WI-FI 802.11 b/g/n, Bluetooth v4.0, Internal Antenna WI-FI/BT, 1 x mSATA, Expansion Connectors (available signals: 1 x USB, 2 x UART, 1 x SPI, 4 x I2C, 1 x MMC, 3 x I2S/McBSP/SSI, 1 x DisplayPort, 1 x 24-bit Parallel Display Interface, 1 x Serial Display Interface, 3 x Serial Camera Interface, 1 x 16-bit parallel Camera interface, 1 x GPMC, 1 x 30 pin Audio Expansion, 1 x 30 pin Battery and Power Management interface, 1 x Keypad matrix. All in a platform with size 135mm x 95mm




VAR-SOM-OM54 CPU: TI OMAP 5 (OMAP5432) System on Module[edit]

VAR-SOM-OM54.jpg

Based on TI’s OMAP 5, 2.0 GHz Dual-Core ARM Cortex-A15. The fastest ARM System on Module on the market today.
The VAR-SOM-OM54 leverages the cutting edge of mobile computing for embedded and industrial product designs. Based on the OMAP 5, 2.0 Ghz Cortex-A15, this new generation System on Module offers a CPU performance boost of over 50% (as compared with the OMAP4460). Supporting 1080p60 video encoding/decoding along with the built-in PowerVR SGX544-MPx GPU, the VAR-SOM-OM54 can handle the most advanced multimedia tasks. It contains: PowerVR SGX544-MPx Accelerated 2D and 3D, 512 - 2048 MB DDR3L and 8GB eMMC, 2 x USB Host and USB 3.0 OTG, Wi-Fi 802.11 b/g/n and Bluetooth 2.1/4.0 BLE. Its size is 51 mm x 67.8 mm x 3.7 mm
