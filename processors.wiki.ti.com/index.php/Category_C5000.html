TMS320C5000™ DSP platform provides more than 30 devices of the industry's lowest power 16-bit DSPs with performance up to 300MHz (600 MIPs). Active power (75% dual-MAC and 25% add) of <0.15mW/MHz and standby power of <0.15mW, helps extend battery life. High peripheral integration and large on-chip memory reduces the overall system cost. The C5000 devices are a perfect fit for a variety of low power and cost-efficient embedded signal processing solutions, including portable devices in audio, voice, medical and biometric applications. With our present and future devices, we plan to extend our ultra low power and value leadership to higher performance.
