Wireless Connectivity

From Texas Instruments Wiki
Jump to: navigation, search

TI makes it easy to connect with SimpleLink™ Solutions. With the industry's broadest wireless connectivity portfolio, TI offers cost-effective, low-power solutions for short range, long range, mesh and IP networks, personal area networks and more. TI Wireless Connectivity devices are helping you connect to the Internet of things. This site includes links to the Official TI Technology Overview Portals where customers can compare or select and purchase or download the right product, documentation, Kits, software and tools. Also, listed below is a comprehensive collection of Wikis providing extensive self-help resources (User's Guides, Getting Started Guides, Testing & Certification information, Software API Documents and more). The table below also provides links to E2E Forums for questions, support and community collaboration with Wireless Connectivity & Internet of Things customers, developers, users and enthusiasts.
