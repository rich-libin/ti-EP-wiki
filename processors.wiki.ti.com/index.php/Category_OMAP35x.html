The OMAP35x portfolio of devices are based on ARM Cortex-A8 core. Combined with graphics capability and DSP technology, these devices create the industry’s best combination of integrated processing capabilities in any single-chip combination. Target applications include: portable navigation devices, portable media players, point of sale, point of services and personal medical equipment.
