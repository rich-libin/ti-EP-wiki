Introduction[edit]

This page serves as a repository of Complimentary Analog and Power Devices that can be paired with the C6455 devices when integrating the device into a system. The Complementary Products listed here serve a wide range of functions such as:

  • Providing basic support functions for the SoC (power, clocking, etc.)
  • Expanding the interface options beyond the on-chip peripherals
  • Creating the link between the digital domains on the SoC to the system's analog/physical domains

The Complementary Analog & Power Products are organized into functional categories, with the goal of showing several options and their respective benefits.

Please visit Texas Intruments homepage for a comprehensive guide to all prducts.

Power[edit]

The C6455 device requires regulated 1.25V core, 1.05, 1.8V, and 3.3V voltages. The table below shows the voltage rails, current, voltage tolerance, and sequencing requirements of the C6455.

TI provide will provide a power spec page on the Analog & Power for C6455 Processor website that gives detailed power specs, power reference designs covering multiple design care-abouts, and other useful links. These power reference designs are built and tested based on the worst case power specs given in the power spec table.

For a complete power solution provided by Texas Instruments please download a full design document already design and tested ready to be used with the C6455 processor. Schematics developed for the C6455 can be found under Reference Designs at link Power Management for C6455.
