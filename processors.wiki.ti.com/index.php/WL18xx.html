Welcome to the WiLink™ 8 Wi-Fi + BT/BLE Main Page.
[edit]

Easily add Wi-Fi and Bluetooth to embedded applications with the certified WiLink 8 module from TI.

WL18xxMOD modules (WL1835MOD, WL1801MOD, WL1805MOD, WL1807MOD, WL1831MOD, WL1837MOD) offer high throughput and extended range along with Wi-Fi and Bluetooth coexistence in a power-optimized design.
TI offers drivers for high-level operating systems, such as Linux®, Android™, WinCE, and RTOS.

Note: All the HW relevant documentation has been moved to ti.com and we are in the middle of migration.
Please refer to: WiLink8 Products Page for hardware related documents.
