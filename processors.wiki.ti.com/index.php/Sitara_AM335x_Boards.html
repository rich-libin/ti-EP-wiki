KA-RO,TX48 - 720 MHz Cortex-A8 System on Module[edit]

Ka-Ro-AM335x.jpg

The Ka-Ro TX48 is based on the AM3354 processor from Texas Instruments and part of the Ka-Ro electronics' Computer-on-Module (CoM) family. Thanks to this family concept, the TX48 is pin-compatible with all the other Ka-Ro TX modules and the Starter-Kit so that application designers are able to develop scalable and flexible, future-proof solutions and achieve fast time-to-market.

Ka-Ro's âTX48 'Made in Germany' module has a clock speed of 720 MHz and a convincing price/performance. With its compact size of only 68 mm x 26 mm (SO-DIMM) it is suited to applications in industrial environments thanks to its extended temperature range of - 40 to + 85Â°C. 256 MB DDR3 SDRAM (16 bit) and 128MB NAND Flash ROM provide ample memory and 10/100 Mbps Ethernet and two high-speed USB 2.0 ports as well as 3x 4 Wire UARTs and a generous selection of connections for peripherals, such as CAN, LCD, I2C / PWM, SD-CARD, are standard features of the Aachen-based CoM specialists' module.

Windows CE, Android and Linux are available as operating systems for the Ka-Ro TX48 and the module comes with a long-term availability warranty of 7 years.

MYIR,MYC-AM335X CPU Module[edit]

Myc-am335x-t.jpg

[3]

Measuring only 70mm by 50mm, the MYC-AM335X is a highly-integrated low-cost ARM embedded SOM (System on Module) supporting up to 1GHz Texas Instruments (TI) Sitara AM335x (AM3352, AM3354, AM3356, AM3357, AM3358 and AM3359) ARM Cortex-A8 processors. It has integrated the AM335x processor, DDR3 SDRAM, Nand Flash (or eMMC) and Gigabit Ethernet PHY chip on board and can be served as the core of your embedded system.

Frequency: Up to 1GHz
RAM: DDR3 (256MB/512MB/1GB)
FLASH: Nand Flash (128MB/256MB/512MB) or eMMC (4GB/8GB)
OS Support: Linux, WinCE, Android
Temperature Range: Commercial, Industrial
Dimensions: 70mm x 50mm

MYIR,MCC-AM335X-J CPU Module[edit]

Mcc-am335x-j-mark.jpg

[4]

The MCC-AM335X-J CPU Module is a feature-packed System on Module (SOM) supporting TI AM335x Sitara processors. It is a ready-to-run Linux controller board, supporting -40 to +85 Celsius extended temperature operation and has brought out up to two CAN bus, two Gigabit Ethernet, two USB OTG and up to six Serial ports and more other signals from one DDR2 SO-DIMM 200-pin gold finger expansion interface.

Frequency: Up to 1GHz
RAM: DDR3 (256/512MBytes)
FLASH: Nand Flash (128/256/512MBytes) or eMMC
OS Support: Linux
Temperature Range: Commercial, Industrial
Dimensions: 67.6mm x 45mm

MYIR,MCC-AM335X-Y CPU Module[edit]

Mcc-am335x-y.jpg

[5]

The MCC-AM335X-Y CPU Module is a compact ready-to-run Linux ARM controller board based on TI AM335x series ARM Cortex-A8 Sitara processors which can run at up to 1GHz. It is ideal for various industrial embedded applications and supports -40 to +85 Celsius extended temperature operation in harsh working environment.

Frequency: Up to 1GHz
RAM: DDR3 SDRAM (256/512MBytes)
FLASH: Nand Flash (128/256/512MBytes) or eMMC
OS Support: Linux
Temperature Range: Commercial, Industrial
Dimensions: 65mm x 35mm
