Introduction to F28M35x Concerto™ MCUs[edit]

Concerto™ dual-subsystem MCUs that enable connectivity without compromise

Concerto brings together connectivity and control by combining a ARM Cortex-M3™ core with C2000’s C28x core on to one device. With Concerto, applications such as solar inverters and industrial control can keep the benefits of separating the communication and control portions while maintaining a single-chip solution. Please visit www.ti.com/concerto.
