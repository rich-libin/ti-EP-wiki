WLAN Channels[edit]

The List of WLAN channels is defined by IEEE 802.11. The 802.11 is practically divided to 2 frequency ranges, 2.4 GHz and 4.9/5.0 GHz bands. were each range is divided into multitude of channels. Countries apply their own regulations to support allowable channels and maximum power levels within these frequency ranges.
