MSP Low Power MCUs

The MSP Low-Power MCUs are ultra-low power, mixed signal microcontrollers from Texas Instruments. The strengths of the MSP family lie in the easy-to-learn, C-compiler friendly, CPU partnered with flexible low power modes and intelligent, low-power peripherals. Its versatility is applied across a number of different end-equipments including medical equipment, electricity and sub-metering, and home appliances such as smoke detectors, thermostats, etc... With over 500 parts available, there is likely to be an MSP430 for almost any application.
