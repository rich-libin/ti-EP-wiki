OMAP44xx ARM Cortex-A9 based developer platforms[edit]

PHYTEC (www.phytec.com)[edit]

PHYTEC develops and manufactures System On Modules, Single Board Computer, provides cutting edge operating system implementations and offers personal support services for all stages of your embedded designs. The range of PHYTEC's services extends from delivery of modules and assistance in the design of carrier boards to development, production and assembly of complete devices.


phyCORE-OMAP4460 System on Module (click here)
The phyCORE-OMAP4460 based on ARM® Cortex™-A9 MPCore™ processor supported the power management abilities of the controller in conjunction with a power management IC make it suitable for battery powered mobile designs. A robust design module, compact dimension (40mm x 50mm) and the reference circuits of the Carrier Board are suitable for Industrial applications.
Frequency: Dual Cortex A9 up to 2x 1,5 GHz // PowerVR™ SGX540 for 3D acceleration
Memory: Up to 4 GB NAND and up to 1 GB LPDDR2
Interfaces:USB 2.0 HS Host, USB 2.0 HS OTG // Ethernet, UART, SPI, I2C // SD/SDIO/MMC // LCD Interface: 1x HDMI, 2x DSI // Camera: 2x CSI
OS Support:Linux and Windows Embedded Compact 7 BSPs
Footprint:Dimensions 40mm x 50mm

Variscite([1])[edit]

Variscite develop and manufacture innovative and trusted embedded-solutions. Our engineering experts design a variety of scalable System On Modules. Variscite serves more than 1500 customers in over 50 countries worldwide, delivering a cost sensitive, high performance portfolio that combines interface flexibility with advanced power management.


VAR-SOM-OM44 TI OMAP4460 System on Module ([2])
The VAR-SOM-OM44, offers an ideal solution for a wide range of applications which require rich multimedia functionality as well as high-processing power. This compact, cost effective and low consumption OMAP4460 SoM offers an ideal solution for mobile applications.


Frequency: Dual Cortex A9 up to 2x 1,5 GHz // PowerVR™ SGX540 for 3D acceleration
Memory: Up to 4 GB NAND and up to 1 GB LPDDR2
Interfaces:USB 2.0 HS Host, USB 2.0 HS OTG // Wi-Fi 802.11 b/g/n and Bluetooth 2.1/4.0 BLE
OS Support:Linux and Android
Footprint:Dimensions 38.5 mm x 67.8 mm x 3.7 mm
VAR-DART-4460 TI OMAP4460 System on Module ([3])
This is the smallest System on Module based on TI’s OMAP4460 (OMAP 4) smart multicore processor with dual ARM® Cortex™-A9 MPCores™ running at up to 1.5GHz each. The DART-4460 is ultra- compact (17mm x 52mm x 4.7mm), cost effective and power efficient. It is ideal for a wide range of target markets requiring small form-factor solution, rich multimedia functionality, advanced graphics and video capabilities, together with high-processing power.


Frequency: Dual Cortex A9 up to 2x 1,5 GHz // PowerVR™ SGX540 for 3D acceleration
Memory: Up to 4 GB NAND and up to 1 GB LPDDR2
Interfaces:USB 2.0 HS Host, USB 2.0 HS OTG // Wi-Fi 802.11 b/g/n and Bluetooth 2.1/4.0 BLE
OS Support:Linux and Android
Footprint:Dimensions 17mm x 52mm x 4.7mm
DART Board Single Board Computer ([4])
Compact and feature packed, the DART Board is the perfect companion to the DART-4460 System-on-Module, serving both for development purposes and as a reference for production, speeds up time-to-market by jump-starting the product development and design phases. The DART Board allows easy access to most of the DART-4460 interfaces, further expanding its connectivity and feature-rich capabilities.


TianyeIT ([5])[edit]

TianyeIT is a leading manufacturer of computer-on-module boards and miniature PC systems. TianyeIT's products excel with an advanced set of features, outstanding level of integration, high reliability and affordable prices. Annual manufacturing rate of over 10,000 boards and systems positioning TianyeIT as a key player in embedded market.


CIP411 TI Omap4430 Computer In Package (click here)
The TianyeIt-CIP411 Computer In Package (CIP) supports the dual-core ARM® Cortex™-A9 MPCore™ based OMAP4430 applications processor. It can operates in a -25°C to + 85°C industrial temperature range and has a form factor of only 40mm x 40mm x 2.5mm  with 174mm 0.9 mm pitch  LGA package . upto  32Gbytes  FLASH and 1 Gbytes LPDDR2 RAM . On Module function such as :10/100 Ethernet Controller;Marvell 8787 WLAN/BT; Touchscreen Controller; upto 3 USB HOST and 1 USB Otg and upto 5 Ports Uart with RS485 transmit data Enable signal .  It is extremely well suited for deployment in wireless, industrial, video/imaging, medical and other vertical embedded applications.
