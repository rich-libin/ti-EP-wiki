DaVinci™ Overview[edit]

DaVinci™ video processors consist of scalable processors ranging from affordable targeted ARM9-only only solutions to full-featured SoCs supporting multiple HD video encode/decode channels. Offering a broad range of performance, price and power consumption points to meet just about every need, these processors also include video accelerators, advanced graphics and display capabilities, high-speed connectivity and a rich set of peripherals that are optimized for a broad spectrum of digital video end equipment. Finally, it is supported by a robust set of development boards and software tools allowing for easy evaluation and development of compelling applications.
