TI’s SimpleLink ZigBee® Module Vendors/Development Partners

From Texas Instruments Wiki
Jump to: navigation, search



This wiki page provides Texas Instruments SimpleLinkTM ZigBee® based product developers with direct links to ZigBee Solutions from Texas Instruments’ extensive network of Wireless Connectivity development partners. This extensive network will help you with faster application development of your TI SimpleLink™ ZigBee® based products.

The network consists of recommended companies, RF consultants and independent design houses that provide a series of hardware module products and (hardware/software) design services.

Feibit[edit]


Company Overview

Feibit is a member of the ZigBee Alliance. Since its 2010 inception, Feibit has been focused on in-depth research and to promote the application of ZigBee technology as the core scheme of things. The introduction of first-class technical experts, with a full range of RF test equipment and production lines, Feibit has helped various industries such as intelligent lighting, smart home, smart agriculture and industrial control systems with mature solutions. Feibit is a strategic partner of ZigBee and has installed products in Midea Commercial Air Conditioning and Haier wisdom family.

Radiocraft[edit]


Company Overview

Radiocrafts designs, produces and markets high performance, high quality and cost-effective standard RF modules for use in a variety of wireless short-range applications. The company also provides customer and application specific design and manufacturing services for high volume products, from specification to turn-key delivery. Based on our experience in a wide variety of products and applications, we find the best solution to take your idea to the market at a minimum of time and cost.

Our technology and developments are based on more than 15 years of solid experience in the art of wireless communication. Our experience covers solutions that are used worldwide in a large number of successful products in the consumer, industrial and automotive markets.

RF Star[edit]


Company Overview

RF-star is a leading wireless RF solutions and core components supplier, focusing on low-power RF technology and products. We are the third-party (Independent Design House) of TI , dedicated to provide customers the best quality electronic products solutions and core components, and leading low-power RF technology in the promotion and application in China.

RF-star provides comprehensive embedded solutions and modules covering Sub1GHz & 2.4GHz, tools, & services, to support customers' products requirements. Products include Consumer electronics DAQ and control, Active RFID, AMR and wireless lighting control system, Industrial monitoring and controlling, LPRF wireless sensor networks, Home and Building Automation, RF Remoter etc.

Embit[edit]

Embit[edit]


Company Overview

Embit was born in 2004 as a design center for RF and embedded solutions. The know-how developed in the years ranges over a wide selection of RF protocols with special enphasis on 802.15.4, ZigBee and Wireless MBus. The main aim of the company is to be and to provide solutions as flexible as possible offering open platform designs and fully customizable OEM solutions. The main fields on which Embit operates are home automation, smart metering, industrial communication, environmental monitoring, renewable energy and energy harvesting.

Embit designs and produces high performance and cost-effective OEM radio modules exploiting the potentials of short range wireless technologies such as IEEE 802.15.4, ZigBee, 6LoWPAN and Wireless M-Bus. The company focuses on the development of spread intelligence systems, to make interaction easier and to simplify the management of wireless sensor networks. Embit assists its customers through the development of ad-hoc wireless solutions and embedded systems, starting from the feasibility analysis, design & development of new applications, to the production of pre-competitive prototypes. Services include Radio Frequency Design, Antenna Design, Hardware Design (Analog & Digital), Software development, Embedded ad-hoc Firmware, Prototyping, System testing and production support.
